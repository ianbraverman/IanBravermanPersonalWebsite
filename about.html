Outside of work, I have many hobbies and passions.

Singing And Musical Performance

Music has been an integral part of my life since childhood. From my formative years in middle school to the crescendo of high school, I immersed myself in various choirs, musicals, and competitions. Among my cherished roles were Motel Kamzoil in "Fiddler on the Roof," The Wolf in "Into The Woods," and Enoch Snow in "Carousel."

My passion for music continued to flourish in college, where I actively participated in esteemed groups such as the Rolling Tones A-Cappella Group, Collegium Choir, UCONN Concert Choir, and Marriage of Figaro Opera. One of my most memorable experiences was touring Italy with the Collegium Choir and UCONN Concert Choir, performing in iconic venues including the illustrious Sistine Chapel.

Currently, my opportunities for performing have been limited due to the global pandemic. However, I am currently a member of the Professional Connecticut choir, Voce.

Looking ahead, I have great aspirations to sing and perform more. Now that I have graduated from FullStack Academy, I am eager to embark on new adventures. My goal is to move to a vibrant city and join a choir there, immersing myself in both the local culture and the world of music.

Traveling

Traveling holds a special place in my heart. From my childhood, I was fortunate to embark on memorable vacations with my parents. Highlights include celebrating my Bar Mitzvah in Israel, exploring the sunny shores of Florida, and marveling at the desert landscapes of Arizona.

During my college years, I continued to indulge my wanderlust with exhilarating trips. As mentioned earlier, I joined the UCONN choirs on a captivating journey to Italy, where we performed in renowned venues such as the Sistine Chapel. Additionally, I seized the opportunity to broaden my horizons by studying abroad in Ecuador during the summer of my sophomore year. Living with host families in cities like Saraguro, Cuenca, and Riobamba was not only a lot of fun but also a transformative experience that significantly enhanced my Spanish proficiency.

Over the past few years, I've embarked on several exhilarating adventures. Prior to commencing my journey at FullStack Academy, I explored Europe with a close friend. Our itinerary spanned multiple cities and countries, including London, Barcelona, Madrid, and Paris. Memorable highlights of the trip included attending a Real Madrid soccer game, immersing ourselves in a captivating Flamenco show, and experiencing the timeless beauty of the opera "Rigoletto."

Looking ahead, my wanderlust remains strong. Now that I've graduated from FullStack Academy, I'm eager to embark on another grand adventure, whether it leads me to Europe or South America. Amsterdam, Dublin, and Lisbon are at the top of my list for future exploration.

Magic The Gathering

Magic: The Gathering holds a special place in my heart as my favorite game, and I've been immersed in both competitive and casual play for many years. Introduced to the game by a friend in third grade, I've been captivated ever since, forging lasting friendships along the way.

Throughout high school and college, I delved deeper into the competitive scene, participating in numerous tournaments. Among my proudest achievements was securing second place at an Invitational Qualifier in the summer of 2019. Additionally, I cherished the casual gatherings with friends at local game stores and conventions across the United States, where the joy of the game transcended competition.

Since graduating from college, my passion for Magic: The Gathering has remained unwavering, with a focus on both tournament and casual play, particularly in the Cube and Draft formats. Among my recent highlights, I achieved notable tournament finishes by placing in the top 64 of both CubeCon 2022 and 2023 in Madison, Wisconsin.

Looking ahead, I'm committed to furthering my journey in Magic: The Gathering, both competitively and socially. I continue to compete in tournaments and enjoy casual play with friends. Additionally, I spearhead a Magic: The Gathering cube discord, facilitating weekly meetups within the local community. This year, my sights are set on achieving a top 8 finish at CubeCon 2024!
